My biggest interest currently is video games. Video games allow you to become someone completely different. My favorite game series is the Dragon Age series. The games are action, adventure, and RPG. I love the game series because I love everything about them. The story, the characters, the world, everything! So far, there are three games in the series: Dragon age: Origins, Dragon Age 2, and Dragon Age: Inquisition.

I personally would rank the games as:

  1. Dragon Age 2
  2. Dragon Age: Inquisition
  3. Dragon Age: Origins

I know that's a bit of a controversial opinion to have Dragon Age 2 as my top favorite as it's commonly seen as the worst game of the series due to lack of development time and budget, but I just can't help but think of that game when I think of the series in general. I'm not blind to the many issues the game has, such as the game being seperated into three acts, none of which have much to do with on another, but the game still brings a smile to my face everytime I think of it.
